How the math works

Markup (Build-from-cost mode) adds % on top of your cost: $1,000 +10% = $1,100. Margin (Analyze mode) is profit as a % of the final price. Break-even covers true cost + overhead — quote below it and you lose money. LA 2026 benchmarks are built into the over/under check.
